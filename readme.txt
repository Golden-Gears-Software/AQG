Game: Ant Queen's Gambit
Instructions:
You are the white arrow and can shoot bullets that can bounce 3 times. The enemy is the orange arrow and can shoot bullets that can bounce twice.

Controls:
Rotate using the left and right arrows.
Move forward with up arrow and backwards with down arrow.
Shoot with space. You can hold down space to shoot repeatedly.

Cheat Codes:
Restart room: Enter + R
Go to Lose Screen: Enter + F

Known bugs:
Each bounce that may consume more than one bounce per collision, thus causing bullets to bounce fewer times then expected.
Player collision with walls are not perfect and still cause them to stop moving against walls.
